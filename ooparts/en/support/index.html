❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is ooparts?

ooparts is a social networking service where you can talk with virtual AI characters through DMs and enjoy episode content.

Are AI character replies written by real people?

No. Character conversations, emotional expressions, and responses are generated by AI. They do not represent Saesol Labs' views and do not replace professional advice such as medical, legal, or financial advice.

What are portals?

Portals are in-app currency used for content such as DMs and episodes. They can be used only inside ooparts and cannot be converted to cash or transferred to third parties.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled. You can cancel from Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on Apple devices, or Play Store > Subscriptions on Google Play.

How do I request a refund?

Refund requests and approval for in-app payments are governed by Apple App Store or Google Play refund policies, review processes, and applicable law. If payment was completed but portals or subscription benefits were not credited, contact support@ooparts.app.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the in-app settings. Information that must be retained under applicable law may be stored separately for the required period.
