Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Saesol Labs (the "Company") values the privacy of users of Ooparts (the "Service"). In accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act, we establish and disclose this Privacy Policy to protect users' personal information and promptly handle related concerns.

1. Personal Information We Collect and How We Collect It

1-1. Information collected during sign-up

Collected item Purpose of collection
Email address User identification and account management
Nickname and profile image Displaying the user's profile in the Service
Date of birth or age verification information Verification that the user is at least 14 years old

1-2. Information automatically collected while using the Service

Collected item Purpose of collection
Device information (OS, device model) Service optimization and error analysis
App usage logs (access time, feature usage records) Service improvement and statistical analysis
Payment records Portal purchase and subscription management

1-3. Information provided while using the Service

Collected item Purpose of collection
Conversation content with AI characters (DMs) Maintaining AI conversation context and character memory features
Character affinity data Operating the character relationship system
Characters and episodes created by users Providing user-generated content
Image tag analysis results Blank Canvas feature (image-based conversation). Original images are discarded immediately.

2. Purposes of Using Personal Information

The Company uses collected personal information for the following purposes:

  1. Providing the Service: User identification, AI character conversations, and episode content
  2. Improving the Service: AI model quality improvement, usability analysis, and error resolution
  3. Payment management: Portal purchase and usage history management, subscription renewal and cancellation processing
  4. Customer support: Responding to inquiries and resolving disputes
  5. Legal obligations: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

3. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

As a general rule, the Company does not provide users' personal information to third parties. Exceptions may apply in the following cases:

  1. Where the user has given prior consent
  2. Where required by law

3-1. Entrusted processing

Processor Entrusted task
Google (Firebase) Authentication, data storage, and cloud services
Google (Gemini API) AI conversation generation
Apple / Google In-app payment processing
Stripe Web payment processing

4. Retention and Destruction of Personal Information

4-1. Retention period

Item Retention period Basis
Member account information Until account deletion Providing the Service
AI conversation content Until account deletion or conversation deletion Providing the Service
Payment records 5 years Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc.
Access logs 3 months Protection of Communications Secrets Act

4-2. Destruction procedure

  1. Personal information is destroyed without delay once the retention period expires or the purpose of processing has been achieved.
  2. Electronic files are deleted in a way that prevents restoration.

5. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights:

  1. Request access: Access to the status of personal information processing
  2. Request correction or deletion: Correction or deletion of inaccurate personal information
  3. Request suspension of processing: Suspension of personal information processing
  4. Account deletion: Account deletion is available in the app settings. Upon deletion, all personal information is destroyed immediately.

These rights may be exercised by contacting support@ooparts.app.

6. Measures to Ensure Security of Personal Information

The Company takes the following technical and administrative measures to protect personal information:

  1. Encryption of personal information (TLS in transit and encryption at rest)
  2. Access control management and retention of access records
  3. Operation of security systems to prevent hacking and related incidents

7. Protection of Children's Personal Information

The Company does not collect personal information from children under the age of 14. If we confirm that a child under 14 has registered for the Service, we will immediately delete the account and destroy the collected personal information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  1. This Privacy Policy may be changed in accordance with applicable laws and internal operating policies.
  2. Changes will be announced in the Service 7 days before the effective date.
