Account Deletion

Last updated: February 9, 2026

CryptoBot allows you to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

In-App Deletion

You can delete your account directly within the CryptoBot app:

  1. Open the CryptoBot app
  2. Tap on the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

Data Type Details
Account Information Email address, display name, profile data
Alert Rules All price alerts and notification settings
Portfolio Data Holdings, watchlist, and transaction history
App Preferences Language settings, notification preferences
Important: If you have an active Pro subscription, please cancel it through the App Store or Google Play Store before deleting your account. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription billing.

Data Retention After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • All personal data is permanently removed from our active systems immediately
  • Backup copies may take up to 30 days to be fully purged
  • Some anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained (this data cannot identify you)
  • We may retain certain data if required by law or to resolve disputes
